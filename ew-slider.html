A terrific web system for all café/restaurants to showcase and handle their online food orders

EateryWay is a tremendous web application which was designed and developed by me. It is a customizable web application fits all needs of any type of coffee shops or restaurants. Unlike any other competitive product, I made the back-end components from scratch with Python Flask, a not only reliable but also flexible framework. I intentionally built it with consideration of flexibility because I always knows that different cafes or restaurants have their unique ideas of their online platform. Some of them want extension abilities of their website to accommodate futures business and some of them may need digital marketing abilities, a circumstance they want a dashboard where they can monitor their customer behaviors.
